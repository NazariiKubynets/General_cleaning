How Much Will Sanitation Cleaning Will Cost for You?

The price for commercial sanitation cleaning is between $0.10 to $0.15 per square foot. This equates to $50 to $100 per hour. However, the price heavily depends on the specific cleaning services required and the materials that need to be cleaned. For example, cleaning windows can cost more $8 per windowpane, and polishing floors can cost up to $0.50 per square foot.

Commercially cleaning a bathroom typically costs around $100, while a private office will set you back around $75. Some of the costs for common commercial cleaning services per square foot include:

Tile & Concrete Scrubbing - $0.12 to $0.25
Carpet Cleaning - $0.08 - $0.25
Stripping Floors - $0.3 - $0.5
Burnishing - $0.04 - $0.12
Buffing - $0.04 - $0.12

If you want to know exactly how much a sanitation cleaning will cost your company, feel free to contact General Clean, and after a brief free consultation, we will be able to give you an accurate quote.
